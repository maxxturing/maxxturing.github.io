Selected as Local Councillor Candidate

With my business, The Tribe, growing and profitable, I decided to spend some of my spare time giving back to the local community where I live in EC1.

After unsuccessfully trying to kickstart a local project to improve broadband connectivity in the area and in the process uncovering a scandal in the local council, I joined forces with the Liberal Democrats to run for local councillor in the Thu 6 May 2021 by-election for the electoral ward of Bunhill in the London Borough of Islington.

Highlights of the campaign included:

  • Creating an informational campaign website:
    • Website: votemaxx.com
  • Designing and printing 6.5k z-fold leaflets and stickers:
    • Leaflets: votemaxx.com/leaflet
    • Stickers: votemaxx.com/sticker
  • Filming and editing a campaign video:
    • Video: votemaxx.com/video
  • Raising £1,420 of donations for the campaign:
    • Donate: votemaxx.com/donate
  • Running a digital marketing campaign:
    • Instagram: @VoteMaxx | #VoteMaxx
    • Facebook: @VoteMaxx | #VoteMaxx
    • Twitter: @VoteMaxx | #VoteMaxx

My campaign focussed on highlighting a need for better local represenation and improved accountability within the local council, as well as better health and safety and an improved local environment for residents.
